Welcome back!

Thank you for choosing Noetic Psychiatry.

Please complete the checklist below as part of your check-in.

  • If you haven't yet set up a Patient Portal account, you can get a temporary password from the front desk.
  • Select My Profile from the left-hand menu.
  • Check over your information.
  • If anything is missing or incorrect, select Edit near the top of the page to make any needed changes.
  • Save any changes using the Submit button.
  • Select My Health from the left-hand menu.
  • Click Medical Forms in the drop-down below.
  • Read over each form listed and sign any that you haven't already at the bottom of the page.
Note: Please be sure to sign an Informed Consent for Psychiatric Services form at the start of each visit. Otherwise, we cannot treat you.
  • Click the Sign Out button in the top-right corner of the screen to log out of your Patient Portal.

That's it! Hit the button below to finish up.
